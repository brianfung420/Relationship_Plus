The concept of Relationship Plus is to present games as gifts to friends or partners, thereby enhancing mutual feelings. During the gameplay, these games can spark conversations and add fun to life. Additionally, the platform integrates with social messaging apps, allowing users to easily create games. In the creation process, users can utilize an online digital content editing service similar to Google Forms to effortlessly present shared memories through text, images, and a series of mini-games, embedding them into a browser.

This way, customers can design a story and adventure unique to their small group based on each person's different experiences, allowing the other party to revisit cherished moments and memories during gameplay.
